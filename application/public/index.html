Grapheme to Phoneme Converter

Instructions:

  • Enter words in the text box (no punctional symbols or special characters)
  • Each word is individually translated to a list of phoneme, which will be displayed in a row under the input box
  • The phonemes are encoded in ARPABET symbols, with numbers indicating stress for vowels
  • Hover on a phoneme symbol to hear approximatively what is should sound like
